Ingredients:
  - Bread
  - Peanut butter
  - Strawberry jelly
  - Nutella (optional)
  - Knife

Directions:
  1. Take out two pieces of Bread
  2. Scoop some peanut butter onto the knife
  3. Evenly spread the peanut butter onto the side of
  one piece of Bread
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 for the strawberry jelly (and Nutella if desired)
  5. Put the other piece of bread onto the first piece; Enjoy!
